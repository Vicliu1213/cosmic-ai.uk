# Regime Map

## Common Labels

- risk-on disinflation
- risk-off growth scare
- inflationary pressure
- policy easing tailwind
- policy tightening drag

## Translation Rules

- falling yields plus weaker dollar often support duration and growth
- rising yields plus stronger dollar usually tighten conditions
- gold strength with falling real yields often signals macro easing or safety demand
- if indicators conflict, describe the regime as mixed and event-dependent
