# Breadth Guide

## What Good Breadth Looks Like

- majority of basket names participate
- leaders are not the only names showing strong volume
- laggards are limited and not structurally weak

## Weak Breadth Warning Signs

- one or two mega-caps carry the whole move
- most names are flat or down despite index strength
- volume confirmation is missing outside leaders

## Reporting Guardrails

- use qualitative language if the basket is inferred
- if breadth is mixed, say mixed rather than forcing a bullish conclusion
